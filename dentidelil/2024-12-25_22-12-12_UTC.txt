🔸 What are veneers?
Veneers are thin shells made of ceramic or composite material, specially designed to cover the visible surface of your teeth. They correct imperfections such as stains, cracks, or misaligned teeth.

🔸 Why choose veneers?
✨ Achieve a bright and natural-looking smile.
✨ Quick and long-lasting results.
✨ Perfect for stained, broken, or spaced teeth.
